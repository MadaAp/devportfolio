About Me

As an email developer, I have a thorough understanding of email marketing best practices and the technical skills to create engaging and effective emails. I have experience designing and coding emails from scratch, as well as working with email marketing platforms such as Mailchimp. I am proficient in HTML and CSS, and am comfortable working with BEE Pro drag and drop email editor.

In my role as an freelancer email developer, I’m responsible for creating and sending weekly newsletters, promotional emails, and transactional emails. I worked closely with the marketing team to develop email campaigns that aligned with company goals and brand standards. I also performed A/B testing to improve email performance and analyzed data to identify areas for optimization.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong communicator and collaborator. I enjoy working with cross-functional teams to develop email campaigns and have experience presenting email performance data to stakeholders. I am also detail-oriented and always ensure that emails are thoroughly tested and optimized for all devices and email clients.
